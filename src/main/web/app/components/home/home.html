Diversity of the Indo-Pacific Network (DIPnet) Field Information Management System (FIMS)

 

A primary goal of DIPnet is to develop a web-based database and infrastructure to aid collaboration and the cross-dissemination of published data. We use the DIPnet FIMS to manage and validate data important for popoulation analysis - namely individual genotypes and precise sampling locations.
